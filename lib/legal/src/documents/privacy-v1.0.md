This Privacy Policy explains how HaloNote, Inc. ("HaloNote", "we")
collects, uses, and shares information about you ("Provider") when
you use the HaloNote service ("Service").

**Note about Patient PHI.** Patient Protected Health Information that
flows through the Service is governed by the Business Associate
Agreement between HaloNote and your organization, not by this
Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect from Providers

We collect the following categories of information from Providers:

- **Account information** — name, email address, encrypted password
  hash, role, and (if you enable it) the secret used for two-factor
  authentication.
- **Workspace data** — note templates, phrase mappings, encounter
  defaults, and the auto-generated writing-style profile. These do
  not contain Patient PHI; they describe your preferences.
- **Usage data** — pages you visit, actions you take in the app,
  recording and note counts, error and performance telemetry.
- **Audit data** — authenticated requests are logged with timestamp,
  user, action, and outcome for compliance and security purposes.
- **Device and connection data** — IP address, user agent, browser
  language, and timezone offset.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 2. How We Use the Information

We use the information described above to:

- provide, maintain, and improve the Service;
- authenticate you and protect your account from fraud and abuse;
- communicate operational matters (billing, security, service
  changes);
- comply with our legal obligations, including HIPAA audit logs;
- conduct analytics on aggregated or de-identified data to improve
  product performance.

We do not use the contents of your clinical notes or recordings to
train our own foundation models, nor those of our vendors, beyond
the per-encounter generation described in the BAA.

## 3. How We Share Information

We share information only as follows:

- **Subcontractors and vendors** under written contracts that
  require equivalent protections (BAAs for PHI handling, DPAs for
  personal data). Current vendors include cloud infrastructure,
  database hosting, transcription, large language model, email
  delivery, and EHR integration partners.
- **Compliance and law enforcement** when required by law, valid
  legal process, or to protect rights and safety.
- **Business transfers** in the event of a merger, acquisition, or
  asset sale, subject to the protections of this Policy and
  notification to affected Providers.

## 4. Security

We protect information with administrative, physical, and technical
safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data, including
TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest, role-based access control, and a
six-year immutable audit log.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain Provider account and workspace data for as long as your
account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply
with our legal obligations and resolve disputes. Audit logs are
retained for a minimum of six (6) years to satisfy HIPAA
requirements.

## 6. Your Choices and Rights

- You may access, correct, or export your Provider account
  information at any time from Settings.
- You may request deletion of your Provider account by contacting
  **privacy@halonote.app**; we will honor the request subject to
  our legal retention obligations and the BAA.
- Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights
  (e.g., under the California Consumer Privacy Act or the GDPR);
  contact us to exercise them.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18
through Provider accounts.

## 8. International Transfers

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your
information will be transferred to, processed in, and stored in the
United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in
your jurisdiction.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will
be presented for acceptance on next sign-in; you must accept the
updated Policy to continue using the Service.

## 10. Contact

For questions or to exercise privacy rights, contact
**privacy@halonote.app**.
